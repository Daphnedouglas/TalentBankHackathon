# HireBank: The Continuous Career OS

A comprehensive Career OS designed to transform professional growth from a series of stressful transactions into a continuous 40-year ecosystem. Moving beyond static resumes and black-box job boards, HireBank synthesizes a user’s "Living Portfolio" to map realistic career trajectories, automate tailored applications, and recommend targeted certifications. It empowers candidates to navigate toxic work cultures through psychological company reviews, learn through bite-sized professional video tips, and practice with a human-language AI interview simulator. By prioritizing transparency and long-term mentorship, HireBank bridges the gap between talent and employers, eliminating noise to foster meaningful, right-fit career pivots.
